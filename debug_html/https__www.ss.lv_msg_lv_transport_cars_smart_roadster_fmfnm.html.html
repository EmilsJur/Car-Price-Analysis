A total of 43, 091 Smart Roadsters were produced between August 2002 and November 2005. This includes both the standard Roadster and the Roadster Coupé versions.

The Roadster Coupé is generally considered more valuable than the standard model due to its unique fastback design, extra luggage space, and relative rarity, making it more desirable for collectors.
